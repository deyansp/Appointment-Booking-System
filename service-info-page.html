About

Our Student Advisors are on hand with information and advice about university life. We’re here to provide information and advice during your studies and also for when it’s time to think about moving on. We also offer advice on services outside of university such as registering with a GP.

These are just some of the areas where we can help:

  • Finding accommodation
  • Induction, matriculation and student cards
  • Advice and support during your time at university
  • Specialist support (e.g. careers advisors)
  • Volunteering, the Third Sector and finding employment opportunities
  • Workshops (e.g. CV writing, applying for jobs, interview skills)
